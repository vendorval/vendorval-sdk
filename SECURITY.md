# Security Policy

We take the security of VendorVal and our customers' data seriously. Thank you for helping keep VendorVal and its users safe.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

**Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues, discussions, or pull requests.**

Report privately by email to **security@vendorval.com**. On our public repositories you can alternatively use GitHub's private vulnerability reporting — open the **Security** tab and click **Report a vulnerability**.

Please include the affected component or endpoint, steps to reproduce (or a proof of concept), and the impact you observed. If you have a suggested fix, include it.

## What to Expect

- We acknowledge your report within **3 business days**.
- We triage by severity and remediate under our vulnerability-management SLA, keeping you informed of progress.
- We coordinate any public disclosure with you on a case-by-case basis.
- If you act in good faith and follow this policy, we will not pursue or support legal action against you.

## Supported Versions

Only the latest published release receives security updates. Please confirm the issue reproduces on the current version before reporting.
